Welcome to the Avengers Help Line!

Need a Hulk to move your sofa?

Iron Man: Tech issues? DIY rocket armor questions? He's your guy (as long as he's not at his 4 p.m. martini).
Captain America: Looking for advice on how to be morally upright? Or need someone to open a tough pickle jar?
Thor: Weather forecast a little too sunny? He'll summon some rain for your garden. Or smash stuff, if that's more your speed.
Hulk: Furniture not quite “minimalist” enough? Hulk can help with that. (Just have a backup chair handy.)
Black Widow: Need a master spy to blend in and uncover those deep, dark secrets? She’s also pretty great at fixing minor tech glitches.
Hawkeye: Perfect for those tough-to-reach light bulbs and high-precision darts tournaments. Call now! An Avenger is (probably) standing by—unless the world needs saving... again.
